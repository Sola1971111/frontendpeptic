The pipeline

From reported symptoms to a decision

Transform a patient's reported symptoms into an AI-assisted risk assessment in five steps.

Typical analysis time: under 15 seconds
Step 01

Patient Information

A patient's reported symptoms across pain, digestive, and bleeding categories.

Step 02

Data Preparation

The system validates and prepares clinical data for analysis.

Step 03

Risk Analysis Engine

Multiple machine learning models evaluate ulcer risk factors and clinical patterns.

Step 04

Risk Prediction

A probability score is generated along with key contributing factors.

Step 05

Clinical Recommendation

The system provides a risk-based recommendation to support clinical decision-making.
